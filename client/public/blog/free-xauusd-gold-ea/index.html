What the free gold EA is

A gold EA is an Expert Advisor — software that trades XAUUSD automatically on MetaTrader by following a fixed set of rules. People also call it a gold trading bot or a XAUUSD robot — it is all the same thing. A free gold EA (or free XAUUSD bot) is exactly that, just offered without an upfront purchase price, and it runs on both MT4 and MT5.

The idea is simple: instead of charging you for the robot, the cost is covered elsewhere. That lets new traders test automated gold trading without spending money on the software first.

What you actually get

  • The EA file for MetaTrader 4 or MT5, ready to attach to a gold chart.
  • Automated XAUUSD execution — entries, stops, and exits handled for you.
  • 24/5 operation when paired with a VPS, so it trades while you sleep.
  • Setup guidance so you can install and run it correctly.
  • Demo-first access so you can watch it before risking real funds.

What you do not get — from any honest provider — is a promise of profit. A robot is a tool, and gold trading always carries risk.

How a gold EA can be free

The most common model is broker-funded. You open a trading account through the provider's partner link, and the broker pays them a small referral commission. That commission funds the free EA, so you get the robot at no cost while paying the same spreads you would anyway.

In plain terms: the broker, not you, pays for the robot. You open an account, download the EA, and trade — the software price is simply covered by the referral. It is a transparent, widely used arrangement in the trading world.

Does "free" mean low quality?

Not by itself. Price and quality are separate questions. A free EA can be well-built, and an expensive one can still be over-fitted junk. The only honest way to judge either is the same: run it on a demo account in today's market and watch how it behaves.

Expert insight: ignore the price tag when evaluating a robot. Focus on how it handles volatility, its drawdown, and whether its live behaviour matches any results shown. A free robot you can test costs you nothing to evaluate — which is a real advantage.

How to download and set it up

  1. Open an account through the partner broker link to unlock the free EA.
  2. Install MetaTrader 4 or MT5 and log in with your account details.
  3. Download the EA file and copy it into the platform's MQL/Experts folder.
  4. Restart MetaTrader so the EA appears in the Navigator panel.
  5. Attach it to the XAUUSD chart and enable automated trading.
  6. Test on demo first, then start small and add a VPS for 24/5 running.

Common mistakes
