About XAUUSD Robot

Last updated: July 2026

In short: XAUUSD Robot (BBFx AI) is an independent resource for traders who want to automate gold (XAUUSD) trading on MetaTrader. We provide a free gold Expert Advisor and honest, research-based guides. We are not a broker, not a fund, and not licensed financial advisors — and we never promise profits.

What we do

Gold is one of the most traded and most volatile instruments in the retail market, and automated trading around it is full of noise: exaggerated screenshots, "guaranteed" returns, and robots sold with no verifiable track record.

We built this site to be the opposite of that. Everything here is aimed at helping you understand how gold EAs actually work — where they help, where they fail, and how to test one properly before risking money.

  • A free XAUUSD Expert Advisor for MetaTrader 4 and MT5 that you can test on a demo account.
  • Practical guides on setup, backtesting, risk management, strategy types, VPS hosting and prop-firm rules.
  • Broker and tool comparisons focused on what matters for gold automation: spreads, execution and EA policies.

How we research and write

Our guides are written to be useful, not to impress. In practice that means:

  • We check facts before publishing. Broker conditions, platform behaviour and VPS specifications are verified against current sources rather than repeated from memory.
  • We explain the downside. Every strategy and tool we cover includes its failure modes — over-optimisation, news gaps, drawdown, wide spreads.
  • We avoid hype language. No "secret algorithm", no income promises, no fake urgency.
  • We update content when conditions or platforms change, and date each guide so you know how current it is.

If you ever find something on this site that is inaccurate or out of date, please tell us — we would rather correct it than leave it standing.
